Data Removal, Modification & Pre-Release Status

User-Initiated Deletion: The TDEI interface provides users the ability to remove their data from public view and from within their own organization. If you delete data through the TDEI interface:

  • The data will no longer be accessible to you, your organization, or other users of the TDEI portal.
  • Any applications or analyses relying on that data through TDEI will no longer have access to it.
  • TCAT retains an archival copy of the data for historical, research, and compliance purposes.
  • TCAT retains the right to preserve existing derivative works that have incorporated your data.

Deleted data will not be reintroduced into public datasets unless explicitly republished by the contributor.

Pre-Release Status & Private Data: You may keep data private by leaving it as "pre-release" at the time of submission. Pre-release data will remain inaccessible to the public until you choose to publish it.

TCAT’s Right to Modify or Remove Data: TCAT reserves the right to reject, modify, or remove any contributed data that is inaccurate, misleading, inappropriate, or otherwise incompatible with TDEI’s mission.
