TDEI is a tool developed by the University of Washington's Taskar Center for Accessible Technology (TCAT) to improve accessibility data of sidewalk infrastructure via the collection, aggregation, processing, and sharing of data related to the accessibility features of pedestrian infrastructure.

1. Data Collection and Usage

TCAT intends to collect, aggregate, process, and/or share the data you publish via this portal with third parties who might develop end-user facing tools (for example, trip planners or policy analysis tools) to help those with sidewalk preferences find suitable routes within their local communities.

2. Data Sharing and Licensing

By publishing data on TDEI, you acknowledge that the data you publish may be shared publicly and included in derivative works created by TDEI or others, and that the data you share will be tagged with the email address you have registered with TDEI.

You also agree, to the extent that you assert ownership rights over the data, to assist TCAT/the University of Washington or its assignees to facilitate this sharing, including but not limited to providing TCAT and/or third parties with royalty-free licenses to use said data.

3. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about the ways in which published data will be used, please contact TCAT at help@accessmap.atlassian.net.
